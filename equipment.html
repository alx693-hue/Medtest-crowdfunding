When Testing Failed: Case Studies

These documented incidents demonstrate the critical importance of independent verification

2012

New England Compounding Center

Massachusetts, USA

Incident: Contaminated methylprednisolone injections

Result: 793 fungal infections, 100+ deaths

Failure Analysis:
  • No sterility testing of final products
  • Visible mold in clean rooms ignored
  • Potency testing skipped to save time
Our Testing Could Have Prevented:
  • Sterility testing would detect Exserohilum rostratum
  • Endotoxin testing would show contamination
  • Visual inspection systems would catch particulate matter
2018

Valsartan API Contamination

Global Recall

Incident: N-Nitrosodimethylamine (NDMA) in blood pressure medication

Result: Global recall, potential cancer risk for millions

Failure Analysis:
  • Manufacturing process changes introduced impurity
  • Inadequate analytical methods couldn't detect NDMA
  • No genotoxic impurity testing required
Our Testing Would Have Detected:
  • LC-HRMS detects NDMA at 0.03 ppm
  • Genotoxicity screening identifies risks
  • Process validation catches manufacturing changes
2022

Heparin Over-sulfation Detection

Early Intervention

Situation: Batch of heparin from new supplier

Our Finding: Over-sulfated chondroitin sulfate contamination

Detection Method:
  • NMR spectroscopy identified abnormal sulfation
  • Anti-factor Xa assay showed 180% activity (should be 100%)
  • Capillary electrophoresis revealed impurity peaks
Outcome:
  • Batch rejected before reaching patients
  • Supplier manufacturing process corrected
  • No adverse events reported

Our 7-Step Testing Protocol

1

Sample Receipt & Chain of Custody

Barcoded tracking, temperature verification, and documentation ensuring sample integrity from arrival.

2

Initial Screening

Rapid tests: pH, osmolarity, visual inspection, sub-visible particle count, and basic chemistry panel.

3

Identity & Potency

HPLC with diode array detection confirms identity. Mass spectrometry quantifies active ingredients.

4

Purity & Impurities

Related substances analysis, residual solvents, elemental impurities, and genotoxic impurities testing.

5

Microbiological Assessment

Sterility testing, bacterial endotoxins, microbial limits, and preservative effectiveness.

6

Stability & Compatibility

Forced degradation studies, container-closure integrity, and IV administration compatibility.

7

Final Review & Certification

Data review by PhD scientists, certificate of analysis generation, and regulatory compliance check.
